About This Speed Test

This speed test measures your network performance by:

  • Download speed: How quickly data can be retrieved from the internet
  • Upload speed: How quickly you can send data to the internet
  • Ping: The reaction time of your connection (lower is better)
  • Jitter: The consistency of your connection (lower is better)

For accurate results, close other applications that might be using your network.
